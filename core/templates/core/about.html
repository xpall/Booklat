Dayap National Integrated High School (DNIHS)

Located in Calauan, Laguna, DNIHS is committed to providing quality education and fostering a love for reading among its students.

The Library
Founded in 2020

The DNIHS Library was established in 2020 to serve the students and faculty of Dayap National Integrated High School. Through the Booklat system, we manage our growing collection of books, track loans and returns, and provide a welcoming space for reading and learning.
